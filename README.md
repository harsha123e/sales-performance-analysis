# Sales Performance Analysis

## Business Problem

A retail company wants to understand what factors are driving its sales and profitability. Management needs insights into product performance, customer segments, and regional trends to identify opportunities for growth and improve business decisions.

The objective of this project is to analyze historical sales data using SQL and communicate key findings through a Power BI dashboard, providing actionable recommendations for business stakeholders.

## Business Questions

This analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

1. How have sales and profit changed over time?
2. Which product categories and sub-categories generate the most revenue and profit?
3. Which regions are performing well, and which require improvement?
4. Which customer segments contribute the most to overall sales and profit?
5. Which products have high sales but low or negative profitability?
