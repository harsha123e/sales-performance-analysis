# Sales Performance Analysis

## Business Problem

A national retail company wants to improve both sales growth and profitability by understanding the factors influencing business performance across products, customers, pricing, and regions. While the company has several years of historical sales data, management lacks clear insights into what is driving revenue, where profitability is being lost, and which areas present the greatest opportunities for improvement.

The objective of this project is to analyze historical sales data using SQL, identify meaningful business insights, and present key findings through an executive Power BI dashboard. The analysis focuses on supporting data-driven business decisions rather than simply reporting historical performance.

---

## Business Questions

This project investigates the following business questions:

1. **How have sales and profit changed over time, and are there any noticeable business trends?**

2. **Which product categories and sub-categories generate the highest revenue, and do they also generate the highest profit?**

3. **Which regions contribute the most to business performance, and where are potential opportunities to improve profitability?**

4. **How do customer segments differ in terms of revenue, profitability, and purchasing behavior?**

5. **How does discounting impact profitability, and are certain discount levels associated with declining profit?**

6. **Which products generate strong sales but consistently deliver poor or negative profit, indicating potential pricing or cost issues?**

---

## Project Goals

* Analyze historical sales data using SQL to answer key business questions.
* Identify the products, regions, and customer segments driving business performance.
* Evaluate how discounting influences profitability.
* Highlight opportunities to improve overall business performance through data-driven recommendations.
* Present findings through a concise executive Power BI dashboard designed for business stakeholders.
